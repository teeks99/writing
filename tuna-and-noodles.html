Tuna and Noodles

Ingredients

  • 1 Can tuna
  • 1 Tbsp butter
  • 1 Tbsp flour
  • 1 Cup milk
  • Black Pepper (or white pepper)
  • Egg Noodles

Directions

  1. Cook egg noodles.
  2. Combine butter and flour and cook until melted and combined into a roux.
  3. Add milk a little at a time while stirring continueously.
  4. Keep stirring until it simmers and begins to thicken slightly.
  5. Add pepper.
  6. Add tuna in chunks.
  7. Mix with cooked and drained noodles.
