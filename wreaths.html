Christmas Wreaths

Makes 2 1/2 Dozen

  • 30 Large Marshmallows
  • 1/2 Cup Butter or Margarine
  • 1 Tsp Vanilla
  • 1 tsp green food color (may need more)
  • 3 cups cornflakes

  • red cinnamon hots candy

  • In 3-QT casserole place butter and marshmallows.

  • Microwave at high 3 to 4 minutes until melted.
  • Stir until smooth.
  • Stir in vanilla and food coloring. (Make DARK green)
  • Blend in cornflakes.
  • Drop by teaspoonful onto waxed paper. Shape into wreaths.
  • Add 3 or 4 red cinnamon hots candies to each wreath for a look of holly.

Alternative: Holly bushes -- Don't bother shaping into wreaths
