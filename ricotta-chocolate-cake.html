Ricotta Chocolate Cake with Frosting From Laura Kent

Cake

  • 3c. flour
  • 1 1/4c cocoa
  • 2 1/2tsp baking soda
  • 1 1/2tsp baking powder
  • 1/2tsp salt
  • 2c sugar
  • 1 1/4c veg oil
  • 2tsp vanilla
  • 1c ricotta
  • 3 eggs

  • 2 1/2c water

  • Preheat oven to 350F.

  • Grease pans.
  • Mix dry ingredients.
  • Mix sugar, oil, ricotta, vanilla.
  • Add eggs.
  • Beat in flour mix and water.
  • Bake 35-40 min.

Frosting

  • 1c sour cream

  • 12oz milk chocolate

  • Melt chocolate until smooth.

  • Let cool to room temp then add sour cream.
