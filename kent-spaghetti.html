Kent Spaghetti

Feel free to use this recipe and give it to all your friends and relatives, but whenever you pass it on, just let them know that this is KENT spaghetti...and have them pass that perl of wisdom on to whoever they may serve this to :-)

Ingredients

  • 1 Green Pepper
  • 3 Medium Onions
  • 1 Tbsp Oil
  • 3-4 Celery Stalks (optional)
  • 1lb Ground Beef
  • 45oz Tomato Sauce (3x15oz)
  • 8oz can Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • 1/2 package of Velveeta
  • 3 Tbsp Sugar
  • 2lb Spaghetti

Directions

  1. Dice Green Pepper, Onions, and Celery
  2. In a large saucepan, sautee with oil until browned
  3. Add ground beef, brown it
  4. Add sauce, soup, cheese, sugar.
  5. Cook 15-30 min.
  6. Cook spaghetti, drain.
  7. Combine sauce and spaghetti, simmer 5-10min (be especially careful to not burn bottom)
