Baked Rigatoni

  • 1lb Uncooked Rigatoni Noodles
  • 24oz Prego With Mushrooms sauce (1 jar)

  • 1lb Shreadded Mozzarella Cheese (1x 4cup bag)

  • Boil Rigatoni Noodles, drain

  • Mix cooked noodles with sauce
  • Put into 9x13 glass baking pan
  • Cover with Mozzarella Cheese
  • Bake 20 minutes at 350F
